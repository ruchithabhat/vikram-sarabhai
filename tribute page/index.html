Dr. Vikram Sarabhai

The father of Indian Space Programme

vikram Sarabhai

Here's a timeline of Dr. Vikram Sarabhai

  • 1919-Born in Ahmadabad, India
  • 1947-established Physical Research Laboratory (PRL)
  • 1947-founded the Ahmedabad Textiles Industry's Research Association
  • 1949-founded Darpan Academy for Performing Arts, Ahmedabad
  • 1961-founded Indian Institute Of Management (IIM) and is considered to be the best institute of management in the country
  • 1960-established Vikram A. Sarabhai Community Science Centre (VASCSC) or the Community science centre at Ahmedabad
  • 1967-founded Uranium Corporation of India Limited (UCIL), Jaduguda, Bihar,under the department of atomic energy
  • 1967-founded Electronics Corporation of India Limited (ECIL) to create a strong indigenous base in electronics
  • 1962-established Indian Space Research Organization (ISRO)
  • 1966-Was awarded with Padma Bhushan
  • 1970-Was awarded with Padma Vibhushan
